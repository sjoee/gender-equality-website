Home Page

Gender equality means that individuals of all genders have equal rights, responsibilities, and opportunities. It signifies the absence of discrimination based on gender and aims to ensure that everyone can realize their full potential, irrespective of their gender.


Objectives

We aim to inform people of all ages about gender equality by focusing on the experiences of women around the world. Gender equality involves recognizing and addressing the social, economic, political, and cultural disparities between genders to ensure that everyone can live a life free from discrimination and bias.


Why should gender equality matter to me?

  • Create a safer and more inclusive society
  • It is a fundamental human right, ensure justice and fairness for everyone
  • Pursue interests and goals without being limited to gender roles

How it is affecting women

  • Although working in the job, women are paid 16% lesser than men
  • One in three women worldwide has experienced physical domestic violence
  • 34.3 million primary school-age girs are still out of school
